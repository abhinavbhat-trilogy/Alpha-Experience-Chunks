# Atomic Experiences Breakdown

Breaking each experience into small, focused "function calls" with clear entry and exit points — per Samy's feedback in PT2 meeting.

**Totals:** 23 Parent + 14 Admissions + 10 Registrar + 6 Guide = **53 atomic experiences**

---

## Parents (23 atomic experiences)

### Pre-Application / Marketing

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | **Learn about Alpha** | Arrives at website / hears about school | Understands Alpha's model and differentiators |
| 2 | **Browse upcoming events** | Visits events page | Sees relevant events filtered to their stage |
| 3 | **Register for event** | Clicks "Register" on an event | Registration confirmed, calendar invite sent |
| 4 | **Attend event** | Shows up on event day | Attendance recorded (sign-in) |
| 5 | **Complete post-event survey** | Receives feedback survey (next day) | Survey submitted |
| 6 | **Submit inquiry** | Fills out inquiry form on website | Confirmation received, enters admissions pipeline |

### Application

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 8 | **Create application** | Clicks "Apply" in parent portal | Application created with child info submitted |
| 9 | **Pay application fee** | Navigates to payment section | $100 fee paid, receipt received |

### Shadow Day

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 10 | **Schedule shadow day** | Opens shadow day scheduler | Slot selected from available dates by campus/grade, confirmed, guide assigned, reminders set |
| 11 | **Sign shadow day forms** | Forms assigned (auto or manual) | All required shadow forms signed |

### Offer

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 12 | **Review & respond to offer** | Offer notification received from admissions | Parent reviews offer details + tuition preview, then accepts (proceeds to enrollment) or declines |

### Enrollment

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 13 | **Pay enrollment deposit** | Navigates to deposit payment | $1,000 deposit paid, enrollment initiated |
| 14 | **Sign enrollment forms** | Enrollment forms assigned | All enrollment forms (emergency, medical, directory, etc.) signed |
| 15 | **Review & sign tuition contract** | Admin releases contract with tuition/discounts/financial aid | Contract signed with full breakdown confirmed |
| 16 | **Set up payment plan & co-payers** | Selects payment plan (monthly/quarterly/annual) | Autopay configured, co-payers invited and accepted, split amounts set |

### Enrolled Student Experience

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 17 | **View & update student information** | Logs into portal | Profile, contact info, medical info, attendance, or documents viewed/updated |
| 18 | **View billing & payment history** | Opens billing section | Payment schedule, balance, and invoices reviewed |
| 19 | **Message school staff** | Opens messages | Message sent, conversation started |

### Re-enrollment

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|

| 22 | **Set up re-enrollment payment** | Payment section in re-enrollment flow | Payment for next year configured and confirmed |

### After School Programs

| # | Experience | Entry Point | Exit Point |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 23 | **Register for after school program** | Browses program catalog, selects a program | Registration confirmed, payment set up |
| 24 | **Manage program enrollment** | Views current after school programs | Schedule viewed, or program dropped |
